Falling Star

"Chhh... Sending a distress signal from the space lifeboat."

Falling Star is a game app provided by Tyranno Rocket.

During an exploration mission, the spaceship of the cat captain was destroyed, forcing an escape in a lifeboat. However, the lifeboat is critically low on fuel.

Solve various puzzles by designing and simulating the optimal route to the target destination.
